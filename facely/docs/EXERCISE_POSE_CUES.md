# Exercise Pose Cues

Use these as 1-line “how to match the picture” prompts in the tutorial/preview.

- Alternating cheek puffs — Pose 1: Seal your lips and inflate one cheek with air while keeping the other side relaxed.
- Alternating cheek puffs — Pose 2: Move the air to the opposite cheek (lips still sealed) and hold that side.
- Chin tucks — Pose 1: Sit tall with head neutral and eyes forward (neck long, shoulders down).
- Chin tucks — Pose 2: Glide your chin straight back (not down) to create a gentle “double-chin” and hold.
- CPS — Pose 1: Keep lips sealed and puff both cheeks evenly with air while keeping your jaw relaxed.
- CPS — Pose 2: Keep the cheeks engaged and pucker lips forward into a small “O” without leaking air.
- Eyes and cheeks — Pose 1: Close your eyes, relax your face, and drop shoulders down away from ears.
- Eyes and cheeks — Pose 2: Keep shoulders down and open your mouth wide (jaw drop) without scrunching your face.
- Fish face — Pose 1: Start neutral with lips closed and cheeks relaxed.
- Fish face — Pose 2: Suck cheeks inward toward your teeth and lightly pucker lips (“fish face”), then hold.
- Hunter eyes 1 — Pose 1: Place index fingers at the outer corners of your eyes and gently lift/hold while you focus your gaze forward.
- Hunter eyes 1 — Pose 2: With eyes closed, lightly press/hold near the outer brow/temple and maintain a soft, controlled squint.
- Hyoid stretch — Pose 1: Cross hands on your upper chest and tilt head back to look up, feeling the front of the neck lengthen.
- Hyoid stretch — Pose 2: Keep hands crossed on chest and return head to neutral (chin level), neck long.
- Hyoid stretch — Pose 3: Keep hands crossed and slowly tuck chin down toward chest to stretch the back/side of the neck.
- Jaw resistance — Pose 1: Place knuckles under the jawline and gently resist as you try to lift/close the jaw (controlled pressure).
- Jaw resistance — Pose 2: Make a fist under your chin and slowly open your mouth against that resistance, then hold.
- Lymphatic drainage exercise — Pose 1: Using knuckles, sweep from under the chin along the jawline toward the ear in an upward motion.
- Lymphatic drainage exercise — Pose 2: Using fingertips, gently stroke down the side of the neck toward the collarbone (light pressure).
- Neck lift — Pose 1: Tilt chin slightly up and lengthen the front of your neck (shoulders relaxed).
- Neck lift — Pose 2: Place a fist under your chin and gently press up while you open your mouth slowly to activate the front of the neck.
- Nose massage — Pose 1: Using knuckles, massage from the sides of the nose up toward the inner eye area with gentle pressure.
- Nose massage — Pose 2: Move knuckles lower and massage along the sides of the nose toward the upper lip/nostril area.
- Nose touching with tongue — Pose 1: Extend your tongue upward toward your nose while keeping lips relaxed, then hold.
- Nose touching with tongue — Pose 2: Reach the tongue as high/forward as possible toward the nose (no strain), then hold.
- Sternocleidomastoid stretch — Pose 1: Place a hand on the side of your neck, turn head slightly away, and puff cheeks while holding the stretch.
- Sternocleidomastoid stretch — Pose 2: Switch sides (hand + head turn) and repeat the cheek-puff hold.
- Thumb pulling — Pose 1: Place thumbs inside the mouth at the corners (fingers outside) and keep lips closed.
- Thumb pulling — Pose 2: Gently pull outward at the mouth corners while keeping lips sealed/puckered and jaw relaxed.
- Thumb pulling — Pose 3: Keep thumbs in place and smile while pulling outward to stretch the cheek/mouth-corner area.
- Upward chewing — Pose 1: Tilt head slightly up and keep lips closed with a tall neck posture.
- Upward chewing — Pose 2: With head still slightly up, open your mouth wide in a slow “chew” motion (controlled, not forced).
